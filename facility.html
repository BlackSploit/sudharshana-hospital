Sudharshana Facilities

Our healthcare center is equipped with an array of state-of-the-art facilities to meet the diverse needs of our patients. This includes a fully-stocked pharmacy for immediate access to medications, a highly sophisticated Intensive Care Unit (ICU) for critical cases, advanced operation theatres for surgical procedures, comprehensive laboratory services for accurate diagnostics, a specialized Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) for our youngest patients in need, and round-the-clock Casualty & Emergency Services for urgent care. Each facility is designed to provide the highest standard of care, ensuring that every patient receives the support and treatment they require.

Pharmacy
The medicine for various departments are addressed by our in-house 24/7 pharmacy. The admitted or incoming patients may require medicine at any point of time. We ensure the full medicine support to the patients to address their needs internally. Sudharshana Hospital has got dedicated staff working in different shifts to meet the emergency needs. Our facility provides easy access to the bystanders to collect the medicine from the pharmacy even late hours without delaying the treatment.
